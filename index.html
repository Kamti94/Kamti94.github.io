I am an Electronics Engineer, a Software Developer and Computer Scientist. I live in the windy City of Chicago. It's always cold here! I view my life as a Markov Chain, wherein the edges represent the choices that I have made. In that case, determining the so called goodness / badness of a choice is just a simple inference problem, based on the outcomes (nodes) in this chain. So, when I say that I wish to pursue a career as a Researcher in the field of Computer Science, it is only a decision under uncertainty that maximises the number of the experiences in life that give me joy and satisfaction. I am excited about research in fundamental areas of Artificial Intelligence. I have come to like probabilistic models in general and graphical models in particular, not only because they provide a powerful way to model complex stochastic systems but also from the algorithmic point of view.

Hidden Markov Model based pen for Handwritten character recognition

Designed and developed an Inertial measurement unit (IMU) based embedded system to obtain orientation data a pen with the IMU attached to it. Implemented a simple Hidden Markov Model and trained it to learn and detect sequences in IMU data as representing one of 36 possible characters (A-Z and 0-9).

GITHUB
This was my 6th semester project. Implemented the entire embedded system from scratch, and used probabilistic models in real life applications for the first time.
